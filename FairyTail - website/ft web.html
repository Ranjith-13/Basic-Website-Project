About Us

In this world of 7 billion people, not everyone are born equal. We, FairyTail try to help people by volunteering.

Everybody can be great. Because anybody can serve.You only need a heart full of grace. A soul generated by love.

We, The FairyTail take up the initiative of saving each person from the brim of death due to starvation. Our volunteers collect the surplus food from the parties, wedding halls, restaurants and distribute it to the needy people.

We provide Shelter and Food to the homeless. We believe no child should go bed hungry and that food is essential.

Instead of throwing off the excessive food at your place, what if that gives life to another person? It's time to think. Make a big difference by changing your small perception and we FairyTail invite you to be a part of this community..!

We believe that together, we can change the way we care for children and help to get them into stable, loving, nurturing environments.
